## Training / Optimization Paradigm

## Reward Signal
- Outcome reward: Checklist-aggregated trajectory reward and downstream tau2/BFCL/ToolSandbox performance.
- Process reward: Fine-grained binary checklist criteria create process-like reward signals.
- Execution reward: Tool responses are produced by an LLM simulator rather than heavy executable environments.
- Format/schema reward: JSON/tool output format constraints are filtered and judged where needed.
- LLM-as-judge: LLM-as-a-Judge evaluates checklist items at rollout time.
- Preference/contrastive signal: none central
- KL/regularization: standard GRPO setup; specific KL not central in note
- Step penalty/cost: Sparse assignment but dense criteria; optional step-level backfilled rewards studied.

## Reward Formula or Pseudocode
Checklist item reward fires when an item switches from unsatisfied to satisfied with dependencies met; trajectory reward aggregates weighted checklist rewards across turns/steps/items into R in [0,1].
